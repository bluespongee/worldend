How Many Levels?


Enigma currently has 18 levels split into 3 acts



Act 0 [01-06]

Acts as a tutorial, teaching you the fundamentals

of Enigma such as how to progress through levels



Act 1 [07-16]

Expands on basic concepts & further develops your

intuition & "feel" for the game



Act 2 [17-??]

The first major roadblock. More complex ideas & even

fewer hints make for much less obvious solutions. It

teaches you patience & dedication as the game only gets

harder from here on out
