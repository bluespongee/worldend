The Game

The goal is to progress through a series of levels

Each level has a unique puzzle, solve & progress through each one to finish the game



How to Play

Enigma will teach you everything you need to know as you play along

The learing curve is steep, but stick with it & after a few levels you'll get the hang of it

Just remember - answers are always lower-case & numerical answers are written as digits (1), not words (one)




Why do some levels have google bars?

It means that you might have to google additional information or use an online tool to get the answer




Do you need any extra software?

Some levels require you to use online tools or other software but they're all free, these are things like audacity or paint.net




Can I mute the background music?

Yes but some levels use music as part of the solution so remember to at least check the music every time you reach a new level





---General Advice---

1) A willingness to learn is key - the game covers many fields of knowledge, but each level is solvable even if you initially know nothing about the topic


2) Google & try everything to find the solution, no matter how strange or even if you think it's wrong, you might find exactly what you need by accident


3) To add to this, certain levels contain hidden hints - these can be found when you give an answer that's incorrect but is close/related to the actual answer.
