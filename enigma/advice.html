General Advice


Don't give up so quickly - it's not meant to be easy. Some levels you'll get right away,

others you'll be stuck on for days. It's easy to get frustrated, but that satisfaction of

finally finding the solution is unlike any other feeling & totally worth the effort



Be willing to learn - the game covers many fields of knowledge & you won't know everything

about each one. Every level is designed to be solvable even if you initially know nothing

about the topic, as long as you're ready to be patient, learn & do a bit of research



If you're stuck - google & try everything to find the solution, no matter how strange or even

if you think it's wrong. You might find exactly what you need by accident. This is also a good

habit for later levels that have almost no hints & require a lot of research & trial & error



To add to the above, certain levels contain hidden hints - these can be found when you give an

answer that's incorrect, but is close/related to the actual solution. So again - try everything
